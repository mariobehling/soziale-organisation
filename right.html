Exchange Students Infosite for Macerata and Italy

::AIM::
This website is a project of exchange students from Spain and Germany set up in 2002. We hope to provide some useful information to future foreign students at the University of Macerata or anywhere else in Italy. Information on this site is in Italian, English or German.
